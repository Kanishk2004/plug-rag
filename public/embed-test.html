Welcome to our website!

This is a sample webpage that demonstrates how the PlugRAG chatbot can be embedded into any website. The chatbot widget should appear in the bottom-right corner of your screen.

How it works:

  1. Add the PlugRAG embed script to your website
  2. Configure your bot settings and customization
  3. The chatbot widget automatically appears on your site
  4. Visitors can interact with your AI-powered assistant
